L-Trominoes

Choose a tile. Fill in that tile—by clicking any edge of the tile—with your least favorite among the available colors. This tile will be referred to as "the removed tile". At any point, if you wish to unselect a tile, you may click the "No Color" button and click on any edge of a filled-in tile to unselect it.

Fill in the remaining tiles with 3-tile L-shaped patterns. To ensure you are indeed covering the remaining tiles with only 3-tile L-shaped patterns, the easiest—on the eyes!—approach is to:

  • First, choose a color different from that used for "the removed tile".

  • Second, choose the first set of three tiles you wish to fill in an L-shaped pattern—again, click any edge of a tile to fill it in.

  • From here, I recommend choosing a different color to fill in the next set of three tiles in an L-shaped pattern—it's easier on the eyes when you use different colors!

If at any point you wish to unselect a tile, use the "No Color" button to do so. Continue the above steps until you run out of L-shapes on the board. Is the board completely filled in with color? That's the goal.

$$\text{All boards are of size} \hspace{4pt} 2^{n} \times 2^{n}$$

Choose which size board you would like to work with.
