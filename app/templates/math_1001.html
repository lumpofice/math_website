A group of people are surveyed about which foods they like, between banana, honey, and butter. The results of the survey are listed below the Venn diagram; use them to compute how many people belong to each subset. Hover over the different areas to reveal the cardinality of the respective subsets—hovering over the "Universe" square will reveal how many people in the survey like none of the items. Once you have completed the current setup, click the "Generate Universe" button to produce a new set of numbers.
